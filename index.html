Who we are!

Award winning logistics company based in Africa

Gorilla Logistic Company is a multinational logistic company that operates in six African countries: South Africa, Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Egypt, and Kenya. The company moves cargos receives from manufacturers and supplies and uses three modes of freight services (trucking, shipping, and airline) for making their deliveries. Moreover, the company utilizes other third party trucking services, including Uber Freight, Convoy Connect, Powerloop, and Keep Trucking, to make some of their in-country cargo deliveries.

We are 2015-2020 award winning logistics company. Gorilla Logistics Company operates vastly by delivering on four portfolios of products/supplies, namely: hospital equipment, construction equipment, agricultural equipment, and military equipment. Hence, they serve quite a segmented market, comprising of military bases, agricultural companies, construction companies, and hospitals.
