We believe in the power of Technology

Rapid-fire innovation

We connect shippers and carriers to one of Africa’s largest multimodal transportation networks on a single, powerful digital platform. We will soon be launching GLC Connect™ which will be one of our most forward-looking innovations – an automated marketplace, unique to the industry, that provides efficient, virtual access to GLC's range of transportation services. The technology uses machine learning to become continually smarter at helping shippers and carriers buy and sell capacity. Optimal carriers are sourced for each load profile, leading to the best outcomes for everyone involved.

Shippers will be able to use GLC Connect™ to assign freight to truckload carriers filtered by geography, equipment and price. We’ve redefined visibility as business intelligence, providing a birds-eye view of fluctuations in capacity, spot rates, weather, traffic and other factors that can affect the choice of carrier.

Carriers will be able access the platform through a digital dashboard on the Drive GLC™ app, which displays income opportunities based on geography, timing and equipment specifications. GLC algorithms analyze trends in spot rates, driving conditions and other factors that impact bids.
